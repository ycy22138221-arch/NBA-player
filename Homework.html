My six favorite NBA players

Stephen Curry

"Success is not an accident; success is actually a choice."

Stephen Curry is an American basketball player for the Golden State Warriors. Known for his incredible shooting range and quick release, he revolutionized the game with his three-point shooting. A two-time MVP and four-time NBA champion, Curry is considered one of the greatest shooters in basketball history.
