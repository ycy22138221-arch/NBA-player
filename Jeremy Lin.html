Jeremy Lin

"You can’t let other people define you."

Jeremy Lin is a Taiwanese-American basketball player best known for “Linsanity” during his time with the New York Knicks. His underdog story and Harvard background inspired fans worldwide. Lin later became the first Asian-American to win an NBA Championship. I even bought his jersey when I was young so he’s my favorite player.
